Privacy policy

This privacy policy describes how the Commands & Colors Medieval Map Generator application (later called "Application") uses and collects the information it has access to.


The owner of the Application is Ludomir Strutyński (later called "Owner"). If you have any questions regarding the privacy policy, please use contact form on the page or email ccnmapgenerator@gmail.com.


Application DO NOT COLLECT any PERSONAL DATA, but some information about user in-app activity is saved. Application saved two types of data:

  1. In case of the Application crash, the state of the app is anonymously collected along with the device OS version and model.
  2. Some anonymous statistical information about what are the most used Application settings (Map type, armies values, use water etc.) and functionalities.


All data are stored on a device and are sent when an internet connection is available. Application is designed to work in air-plain mode, and disabling network will not limit any app functionality.


All data are used only to fix bugs and enhance future versions. It will not be disclosed to any 3rd party.


If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact me by contact form or email ccnmapgenerator@gmail.com. I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.


This Privacy Policy was last updated on January 17th, 2020.
